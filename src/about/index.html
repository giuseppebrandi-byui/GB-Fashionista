Our Culture

Mark Twain said, “Whenever you find yourself on the side of the majority, it is time to pause and reflect”. In many ways, GB Fashionista's culture reflects the words of Mark Twain. While we study emerging trends and learn from the top players in the industry, we stand out from the crowd by trying new things and experimenting.

Our fashion designers are insanely curious about everything because they are constantly seeking to be inspired to come up with new ideas. As we live through a time of change, we believe fashion thrives on change since it creates an appetite for change.
